Features for Reps
Feature 01 — Intelligent Order Capture

Customer texts an order. Pesto handles the rest.

  • Captures orders from SMS, calls, voicemails, emails, and photos
  • Parses natural language and matches items using customer history
  • Clarifies ambiguities with the customer on the rep's behalf
  • Auto-populates cart in your ordering system
  • Rep reviews and submits with one tap
  • Flags items they usually order but didn't mention
Feature 02 — Competitor Price Comparison

Win the price conversation. On the spot.

  • Snap a photo of any competitor invoice
  • AI matches items to your catalog and generates a side-by-side comparison in seconds
  • Normalizes units and flags misleading pack-size differences
  • Generates a shareable savings summary for the customer
Feature 03 — Interaction Transcription

Walk in. Talk. Walk out with everything done.

  • Transcribes in-person visits, calls, and voicemails automatically
  • Generates structured notes by topic — no post-meeting write-ups
  • Extracts action items, commitments, and follow-up tasks
  • Flags sales opportunities from conversation content
  • Syncs notes and tasks directly to CRM

Feature 04 — Shorts & Substitutions

Your customer knows the moment the warehouse does.

When a short is flagged, Pesto notifies the customer immediately — even before the rep's day starts. It suggests substitutions based on their order history, not defaults. Once confirmed, it handles the changes automatically or loops in the rep.

Feature 05 — Order Guide Generation

New account? Pesto builds the guide for you.

Take a customer's menu, inventory list, or a conversation about their needs — and Pesto generates a tailored order guide matched to your catalog, ready to hand to the customer. No more searching AS400 line by line.
