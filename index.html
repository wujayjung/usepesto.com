Feature 01 — For Reps

Intelligent Order Capture

Customers text, call, or leave a voicemail. Pesto intercepts it, parses the natural language into exact catalog items, and has a cart ready for one-tap review and approval.

  • Captures orders from SMS, calls, voicemails, and in-person conversations
  • Matches vague requests to exact SKUs using order history ("the usual chicken" → Tyson #4472819)
  • Flags missing items the customer usually orders but didn't mention
  • Rep reviews, adjusts, and submits with a single tap
Feature 02 — For Reps

Instant Competitor Price Comparison

A customer pulls out a competitor invoice and says "I'm getting a better deal." The rep snaps a photo and Pesto delivers a full side-by-side comparison in seconds — using what the customer actually pays, not list price.

  • Snap a photo of any competitor invoice, regardless of format
  • AI extracts every line item and matches it to your catalog
  • Uses customer-specific pricing for an accurate comparison
  • Surfaces where you win, where you're competitive, and where to request deviations
Feature 03 — For Reps

Customer Interaction Transcription

The rep selects a customer, taps record, and walks into the meeting. Pesto listens, understands, and generates notes, action items, opportunities, and CRM-ready output — automatically.

  • One-tap recording runs silently in the background during visits
  • Full searchable transcript with meeting summary and key takeaways
  • Orders and requests extracted and fed directly into order capture
  • CRM-ready notes, tasks, and opportunities — no write-ups required

Feature 04

Shorts & Substitutions

An ordered item can't be delivered. With Pesto, the customer knows before the truck arrives — not after. The agent detects the short, suggests a substitution, and handles the back-and-forth.

  • Detects shorted items through inventory system integration or manual shorts reports
  • Matches the best available substitute based on category, brand, and customer preferences
  • Notifies the customer proactively via their preferred channel (text, email, app)
  • Routes the customer's approve/decline response back to the warehouse and rep simultaneously
Feature 05

Order Guide Generation

Opening a new account? Pesto builds the full order guide automatically from the customer's menu, preferences, and conversations — no more manual AS400 lookups or back-and-forth with purchasing.

  • Ingests the customer's menu (photo, PDF, or URL) to identify what they need
  • Maps menu items to catalog products with correct item numbers, pack sizes, and real pricing
  • Produces a clean, editable PDF order guide on the spot
  • Updates automatically as ordering patterns develop post-onboarding
